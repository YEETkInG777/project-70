Mad Science

Was established in 2001

Loop Lab, our STEM subscription box, contains experiments that focus on fun, interactivity, and entertainment! Kids 8+ can perform 3-4 experiments per box, with themes including Gross Science, Spy Science, and Sense-ational Science! Available in the US only.

school library

Was established in 2001

The role of the school library at École Julie-Riel is to support teaching and learning goals that ensure students develop as information literate learners. The tools for realizing those goals are designed around active learning environments, access to information sources, the use of inquiry-based teaching and learning, and collaborative teaching and learning.

school live events

Was established in 2001

During the Early Years, emphasis is placed on the acquisition of basic movement skills and their use in introductory and simple activities. Students begin to understand what movements to do, as well as why and how to do them. These skills are the building blocks for participation in all physical activity and are the prerequisites for lifelong physical activity.
